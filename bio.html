☹Scattered thoughts. Me?☺;

I was born Christian O'Neal McMillan, 1997, January 11th. Since a child I have had a mind that wandered.
As I aged I began to question everything around me and never took anything at face value.
If you see something in a particular light, I'm trying to turn the light off and feel it out.
This has always been my mindset. I found my first passion as a freshman in highschool, music, specifically hip hop.
Since then I've immersed myself in the culture, current and previous generations.
My junior year I decided to start making music on my own. Thats when I started writing songs
that werent just pointless words that rhyme, it was a start. A year later I became interested with
mixing my own vocals and audio. This year I started producing, making the whole thing from scartch all by myself.
